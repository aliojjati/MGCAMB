Cosmological Tests of Modified Gravity

By A. Hojjati, G.B. Zhao, A. Zucca, L. Pogosian and A. Silvestri


We provide a set of patches to the publicly available codes CAMB and CosmoMC allowing them to work with different models of Modified Gravity.

The use of our patches is free, however, we request that you cite the following two papers: Testing Gravity with CAMB and CosmoMC and Searching for modified growth patterns with tomographic surveys , as well as the original CAMB and/or CosmoMC papers.
